My Favorite Food


My Favorite food is Veg Burger🍔🍔 with Extra Cheese.
Veg Burger is an assembly of Mayo,Tomatoes,Potato cake,Onions, mouth-watering Cheese slices
and some Organo-leaves garnishing inside the two cut pieces of a Bun.The same is cooked
keeping in microwave for few minutes
in order to get intense the flavours.
I love the combination of different flavours and
especially the flavour of Yumm Cheese mixed with that of Onions😋.
Secondly,The mixture of Salty, Sweet and Sour taste which comes
from in a Bite of Potato cake and Tomatoes.
Lastly the sprinkled Organo leaves
enhances its worth of being a Mouth-watering Dish.
I really love burgers🍔🍔 a lot and especially the one I prepare
at home with some simple tricks and ingredients.
I'll be sharing my special recipe with you hoping you too find it Delicious.

Ingredients Required

  • Buns(as many burgers you want).
  • Veggies like :
    • Tomatoes
    • Potatos
    • Green Chillies
    • Onions
  • Spices like :
    • Red-Chilli Powder
    • Salt
    • Organo Leaves
  • Butter
  • Cheese Slices
  • Mayo and
  • Tomato Sauce

🍔Steps to Prepare👩‍🍳:🍔

  1. Preparation of Potato Cake
  2. Mash the boiled potatoes + (two or three)in a bowl and add :
    • Salt to taste.😋
    • Little Red-Chilli powder.😋
    • Chopped veggies like Onions and Green Chillies. 😋
    • Mix them well and shape them like small flattened cakes😋.
    • Make them as many burgers you want and place them on a pre-heated pan
      containing butter😋 till they appear brownish😋.
    • Cook them properly on both sides and take out of the pan😋.
  3. Cut the bun in two halves with a knife and apply butter on both😋.
  4. Place both the pieces in Microwave oven for 5 minutes.
  5. Take them out carefully and on the base portion:
    • Apply some Mayo and a Cheese Slice.😋
    • Now place Tomato slices🍅(one or two).
    • Place previously prepared potato cake.😋
    • Then repeat Step1 and after that place Onion Slices (one or two) upon it. 😋
    • Now, Place Cheese Slices upon it.😋
  6. Finally, place upon it the upper cut portion of the Bun.😋
  7. Place whole arrangement🍔 into Oven for 2 minutes.
  8. And You are Done🍔🍔😋. Take it out and serve with Tomato Sauce.😉

Hope you loved the easy steps and the taste.
